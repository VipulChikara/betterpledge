Frequently Asked Questions

What is Better Pledge?
Better pledge is a web platform made by Google Student Community, DITU where individuals can donate surplus food from restaurants and communities to serve the less fortunate. The NGO’s can run through the platform whenever they require food for the less fortunate.
How did this start?
It started as project in the esteemed Google Student Community of DIT University but it grew with support of individual donors, hotel and restaurant owners and NGO’s.
Who are we?
We are students of DIT University, Dehradun.
What about funds?
We are a zero funds organization. Better pledge has no revenue, employees, nor office space – if you want to help just list the food items you want to donate and NGO’s will surely get up to you.
How did you grow without money?
Simple – we are in the business of connecting peoples to serve the less fortunate. We share our experiences on social media. Facebook, Instagram and WhatsApp are our tools to grow.
I liked the concept – how can I help?
Easy – we need you to just create an id on the website list the food items you want to donate that’s it the NGOs will reach out to you, you just need to provide the information of the food and where to collect it from.
Do you provide certificates?
Nah – Our mission is to reduce the food hunger index and help the less fortunate.
How can I donate?
Thanks for wanting to support us –better pledge DOES NOT accept money. We have grown through contributions in kind and partnerships.
I am in journalism – how can I contact?
Thanks for the interest – hopefully this will help us spread us reach to more donors and NGO’s, more cities, and more people. Do write to us at betterpledge@gscditu.com
